Grain

The grain feature is often used when wanting to give a photo an "old-styled" theme. It makes photos more pixelated just as they were in the past.

Saturation

Tinting

A tinted photograph is a photograph produced on dyed printing papers. This used to be done a lot in the past, through commercial manufacturers or a hand-colored photograph. However, now people simply edit photos through technology. It is when a single overall colour underlies the photograph.

Cropping

Cropping a photo reduces the photo size. It often cuts down parts of the image from different sides of the photo.

Vignette

Vignette is a feature used to edit photos which creates darkness around the photo. It can be used when an entire photo is relatively bright, or when it is necessary to focus on the middle of the photo.
