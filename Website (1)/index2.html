Long Exposure

Through long-exposure, people capture photos with a slow-shutter. This helps to sharply capture the stationary elements of images while blurring or obscuring the moving elements.

Forced Perspective Photography

Forced perspective is a technique which depicts optical illusions in order to make an object appear farther away, closer, larger or smaller than it actually is. In other words, it is a photo which may be deceiving to the eye.

Macro Photography

Macro Photography captures images which are difficult to be seen in detail by the human eye. They are typically taken of insects.

Panoramic Photography

Panoramic Photography uses specialized equipment or software in oder to capture photos with horizontally elongated fields of view.

Reflection Photography

Reflection photography also known as mirror photography, is when one uses reflective surfaces to create an artistic echo of a scene. It can be done of nature and people.
